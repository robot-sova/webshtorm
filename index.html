FAQ

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

Common causes include low refrigerant due to a leak, dirty condenser coils, a failing capacitor, thermostat settings, or restricted airflow (clogged filter, icing, or blower issues).

Is short cycling a big deal?

Yes. Frequent on/off cycling increases wear and can raise energy bills. It may be caused by sensors, airflow problems, overheating, control issues, or refrigerant-related faults.

What's included in a maintenance tune-up?

We inspect key components, check electrical connections, clean accessible coils, clear/inspect drain lines, test performance, and help prevent surprise breakdowns.

Do you work on mini-split systems?

Yes. We handle leaks, drainage issues, weak airflow, errors, noise, and general maintenance for indoor and outdoor units.
